<!> If you don't want grass to grow naturally (as it will, by default, based on season), do this: <!>
========================================================================================================================================================================================================
Also note: if you use this datapack with Cascades, be sure to put it above, to take precedence!
========================================================================================================================================================================================================
1. Navigate to: ".minecraft/config/iguanatweaksreborn/common.toml"

2. set this config to false:

		"Grass Decay and Growth" = false
========================================================================================================================================================================================================
Or, even easier...

1. Click "Mods" from the title screen and navigate to: "Iguana Tweaks Reborn/iguanatweaksreborn/common.toml/Edit Config/World/Seasons", and turn it OFF.
