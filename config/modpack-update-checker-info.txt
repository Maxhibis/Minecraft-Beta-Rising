Embark on a journey through time reborn in Beta Rising, a new modpack meticulously crafted with passion to bring back the feeling of "old Minecraft", up to date in the latest version 1.20.1. 

Beta Rising is adventurous in it's nature, basing it's core gameplay around the version beta 1.7.3, it reimagines and improves this classic version anew, without sacrificing it's nostalgic charm.
